The Process

Four steps to your door.

We handle every detail on the ground in China so you can focus on selling.

Step 01

📋

Tell Us What You Need

Share product requirements, target price, and order volumes. We scope the brief and confirm what's achievable in Yiwu.

Step 02

🤝

Supplier Matching

We walk the market, vet suppliers face-to-face, and shortlist the best options — negotiating prices and MOQs on your behalf.
